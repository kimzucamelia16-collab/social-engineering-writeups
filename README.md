 ## Exercise 1: Phishing Emails in Action (TryHackMe)


### Objective
The objective of this exercise is to understand the structure of an email and identify phishing indicators through email header and content analysis.

### Key Concepts Learned
- Difference between email header and email body
- Purpose of email header fields such as `From`, `Return-Path`, `Received`, and IP address
- How attackers spoof sender information
- Common phishing indicators in emails

### Phishing Indicators Identified
- Suspicious sender domain
- Mismatch between sender and reply-to address
- Unknown or suspicious sending IP address
- Urgent or threatening language
